Synopsis

Mindful East/West explores the complexities and richness of mindfulness practices in the U.S. and India. Scheduled for release in May 2017, the documentary shows a wide range of practices, from Tibetan monks meditating in the caves of the Himalayas to school children in trauma-informed schools in the western U.S.
