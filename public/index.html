From Himalayan Caves to American Schools...

In the United States, mindfulness practice has become increasingly integrated into industry and public service, and quickly: today, millions incorporate mindfulness into their daily lives to aid in trauma intervention, to achieve peak performance, and to reduce stress.

The roots of this practice lie in India, where Tibetan Buddhist monks and nuns consider mindfulness a way of life.

Mindful East & West documents the path this practice has taken from its purest form in India, to its many adapted versions in American culture.

Our film illustrates the impact mindfulness has had upon both American and Indian lives, and ultimately, the transformative power of this practice.
